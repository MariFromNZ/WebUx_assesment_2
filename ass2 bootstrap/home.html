Drivers License Requirements

The minimum rental age is 21 years.

For renters under 25 years, additional charges may apply and could be paid at the location.

NZ law requires a current foreign driver’s licence in another language also be accompanied by an English translation or an International Drivers Permit. This translation must be provided by an authorised service or nominated authority.
